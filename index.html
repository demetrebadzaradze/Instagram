Data Deletion Instructions

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All information accessed via the Instagram API is used temporarily within the app session only.

If you wish to delete your data or remove our app's access to your Instagram account, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Instagram account via a web browser or the mobile app
  2. Navigate to Settings > Apps and Websites
  3. Find our app API BUNDLER in the Active tab
  4. Select it and click Remove

This will immediately revoke our access to your Instagram account and remove all session data from our app.
